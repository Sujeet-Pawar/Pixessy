Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how Pixessy collects, uses, and protects your personal information. By using our website and services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Data We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address when you create an account or place an order.
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details or other payment methods to process your transactions securely.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you use our website, such as pages visited, time spent, and interaction patterns.
  • Device Information: Details like IP address, browser type, and device type to enhance your user experience.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data for various purposes, including:

  • Order Fulfillment: To process and deliver your orders, and to communicate with you regarding your purchases.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, provide assistance, and improve our support services.
  • Personalization: To tailor your experience by suggesting products and offers based on your preferences.
  • Marketing: To send you promotions, offers, and updates if you have opted in to receive marketing communications.
  • Analytics: To understand how our services are used and make improvements.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist us with payment processing, delivery, and customer support.
  • For Legal Compliance: When required by law, we may disclose your information to comply with legal processes or to protect our rights.
  • In Business Transactions: If we undergo a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred to the new entity.

Data Security

We implement strong security measures to protect your data. Our systems use encryption, secure servers, and access controls to safeguard your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request corrections if your data is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing your data under certain circumstances.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@pixessy.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
